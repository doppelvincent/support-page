Data Protection

Expense Guardian – Privacy & data handling

1. Local-only storage

Expense Guardian stores all your data only on your device. Your expenses, amounts, dates, reminders, and any other information you enter never leave your device and are not sent to any server or to the developer.

As the developer, I do not have access to your data. I cannot see, collect, or process your expense information.

2. Data we do not collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or store your expense data anywhere other than on your own device. No analytics, usage tracking, or personal data is sent to me or to third parties through the app.

3. Your data, your control

You have full control over your data. It stays on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app. There is no cloud backup or sync to my systems, so I have nothing to retain or delete on your behalf.

4. This website

This support website may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality. If you contact me by email (for example for support), I will process only what you send in that message to respond to you.

5. Changes to this notice

This data protection notice may be updated from time to time. The “Data protection” page on this site will always reflect the current version.
